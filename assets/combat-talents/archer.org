*** Archer
If you wield a bow....

- [-] When you [[file:~/other/ironsworn/moves/adventure/secure-advantage.org][Secure an Advantage]] by taking a moment to aim, choose your approach and add +1.
    - Trust your instincts: Roll +wits, and take +2 momentum on a strong hit.
    - Line up your shot: Roll +edge, and take +1 momentum on a hit.

- [ ] Once per fight, when you [[file:~/other/ironsworn/moves/combat/strike.org][Strike]] or [[file:~/other/ironsworn/moves/combat/clash.org][Clash]], you may take extra shots and suffer -1 supply (decide before rolling). When you do, reroll any dice. On a hit, inflict +2 harm and take +1 momentum.

- [ ] When you [[file:~/other/ironsworn/moves/adventure/resupply.org][Resupply]] by hunting, add +1 and take +1 momentum on a hit.
