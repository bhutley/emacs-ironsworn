*** Weaponmaster
Once you [[file:~/other/ironsworn/moves/quest/fulfill-your-vow.org][Fulfill Your Vow]] (formidable or greater) in service to a seasoned warrior, and [[file:~/other/ironsworn/moves/relationship/forge-a-bond.org][Forge a Bond]] to train with them...

- [-] When you [[file:~/other/ironsworn/moves/adventure/secure-advantage.org][Secure an Advantage]] by sizing up your foe in a fight, or in a charged situation which may lead to a fight, add +1 and take +1 momentum on a hit.

- [ ] When you study or train in a new weapon or technique, you may obtain and upgrade that combat talent for 1 fewer experience.

- [ ] When you [[file:~/other/ironsworn/moves/combat/turn-the-tide.org][Turn the Tide]] with a sudden change of weapon or technique, and your next move is a [[file:~/other/ironsworn/moves/combat/strike.org][Strike]], add +1 and inflict +2 harm on a strong hit.
