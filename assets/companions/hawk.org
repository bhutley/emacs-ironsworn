*** Hawk
Name:

Your hawk can aid you while it is aloft.

- [ ] *Far-seeing:* When you [[file:~/other/ironsworn/moves/adventure/undertake-a-journey.org][Undertake a Journey]], or when you [[file:~/other/ironsworn/moves/adventure/resupply.org][Resupply]] by hunting for small game, add +1.

- [ ] *Fierce:* When you [[file:~/other/ironsworn/moves/adventure/secure-advantage.org][Secure an Advantage]] +edge using your hawk to harass and distract your foes, add +1 and take +1 momentum on a hit.

- [ ] *Vigilant:* When you [[file:~/other/ironsworn/moves/adventure/face-danger.org][Face Danger]] +wits to detect an approaching threat, or when you [[file:~/other/ironsworn/moves/combat/enter-the-fray.org][Enter the Fray]] +wits against an ambush, add +2.
