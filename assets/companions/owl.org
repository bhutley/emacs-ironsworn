*** Owl
Name:

Your owl soars through the darkness.

- [ ] *Nocturnal:* If you [[file:~/other/ironsworn/moves/adventure/resupply.org][Resupply]] at night by sending your owl to hunt, take +2 momentum on a hit. When you [[file:~/other/ironsworn/moves/combat/enter-the-fray.org][Enter the Fray]] +wits against an ambush in darkness, add +1 and take +1 momentum on a hit.

- [ ] *Sage:* When you leverage your owl's secret knowledge to perform a ritual, add +1 or take +1 momentum on a hit (decide before rolling).

- [ ] *Embodying:* When you [[file:~/other/ironsworn/moves/suffer/face-death.org][Face Death]], take your owl's health as +momentum before you roll.
