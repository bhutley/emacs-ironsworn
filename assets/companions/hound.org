*** Hound
Name:

Your hound is your steadfast companion.

- [ ] *Sharp:* When you [[file:~/other/ironsworn/moves/adventure/gather-information.org][Gather Information]] using your hound's keen senses to track your quarry or investigate a scene, add +1 and take +1 momentum on a hit.

- [ ] *Ferocious:* When you [[file:~/other/ironsworn/moves/combat/strike.org][Strike]] or [[file:~/other/ironsworn/moves/combat/clash.org][Clash]] alongside your hound and score a hit, inflict +1 harm or take +1 momentum.

- [ ] *Loyal:* When you [[file:~/other/ironsworn/moves/suffer/endure-stress.org][Endure Stress]] in the company of your hound, add +1.
